Comparing Shaders for MC 1.21

Lately, I've been wanting to test around with some shaders - so why not? Problem? I use a laptop with integrated graphics. Yup.

Fix? Compare 3 popular "potato" shaders and see which is better.

So.. here it is!

If you're wondering why it's out of 6: I limited my FPS to 60, that's all!

1. Mellow - 4.375
